THE FILM’S SUBJECTHenri Nouwen · 1932–1996
A man who had spent his last years writing about a single painting.
He thought he was
the messenger.
The film he was trying to make
was, slowly,
being made on him.
i. the climb
A man who had climbed
every ladder he could find.
From the outside it looked like succeeding.
He knew all the words.
Grace.
Mercy.
Love.
He just couldn’t quite — feel them.

2021.

TARIFFVILLE · NOV. 2021The kind of New England day where the sun gives up by four.
The room was thin.
A thin one… for a life that had not been thin.
at the back of the church
My younger brother walked in.
Late.
Six-foot-four. Gaunt. He took the podium and spoke for forty-five minutes.
And I felt
the shame of my entire life
rise up and meet me.
That is where
the climbing ended.
ii. the wilderness
Three years.
Looking for a way out, and not finding one.
The wilderness took the ladders.
It took the words.
The way Hemingway said things go bankrupt.
Gradually.
And then suddenly.
iii. the film
I started to remember
the film.
